SureWaka is a technology-driven logistics platform built for Nigeria. We connect people and businesses who need to move goods with verified logistics providers and independent drivers — all from one interface.

Nigeria's logistics market is fragmented. Customers juggle dozens of disconnected carriers with no price transparency, no real-time tracking, and no single place to compare and book. SureWaka fixes that.

We operate two models on one platform:

Carrier aggregation — registered logistics companies (like GIG, DHL) list their services, and customers compare rates and book instantly.
On-demand matching — individual drivers and fleet owners get matched to nearby customers in real-time for last-mile and urgent deliveries.
We don't own vehicles. We build the technology layer that makes the market work — booking, matching, payments, KYC verification, and a ratings system that keeps quality high on both sides.

Who we serve first: SME e-commerce sellers and everyday senders in Lagos who need reliable, affordable shipping and can't get it today.

How we make money: Commission on every completed delivery, service coordination charges, and premium/priority delivery fees.

Where we're headed: Launch in Lagos, expand to 3 more Nigerian cities in Year 1, and scale to 520,000 users and 22,000 driver partners by Year 3. UK expansion comes later — only after Nigeria is proven.
